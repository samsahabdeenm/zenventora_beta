Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Zenventora collects, uses, shares, and protects information submitted through our website, enquiry forms, partner conversations, and customer solution requests.

Last updated: 26 June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect name, company name, email address, phone number, requirement type, message details, product or service information, and any files or links shared voluntarily for partnership or customer support discussions.

2. How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to enquiries, understand product partnerships, coordinate customer requirements, arrange demos or introductions, improve website content, and maintain business communication records.

3. Partner and Customer Requirement Data

When a company or customer contacts Zenventora, we may use the shared details to evaluate fit, route the enquiry internally, or connect the request with a suitable product or solution provider after reasonable business review.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share relevant enquiry details with internal team members, service providers, or suitable partner companies only when it is necessary to respond to a request, coordinate a solution, or comply with legal obligations.

5. Cookies and Analytics

The current template does not require tracking cookies by default. If analytics, pixels, chat widgets, or third-party scripts are added later, this policy should be updated to explain what data is collected and how users can manage preferences.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and communication records only as long as reasonably required for business follow-up, partnership review, support, compliance, dispute handling, or legitimate operational purposes.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. However, no website, email, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Choices

You may request correction, update, or removal of your submitted details by contacting us. We may retain limited records where required for legal, security, or legitimate business reasons.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to partner websites or third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, and Zenventora is not responsible for external website content or practices.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact support@zenventora.in, call 8072009245, or message us on WhatsApp.
